Redesign the entire POS (Point of Sale) page into a premium modern cashier experience.

IMPORTANT:
This is NOT an admin dashboard.
This page is used continuously by cashiers for 8–12 hours every day.
Everything must prioritize speed, clarity, and comfort while still looking premium.

Follow exactly the same design language already used in the new Sales Home page and Sidebar.

DO NOT change any business logic.
DO NOT change routes.
DO NOT change APIs.
DO NOT remove existing functionality.
Only redesign the UI/UX.

====================================================
GLOBAL DESIGN LANGUAGE
====================================================

Use the Risol Yuk design system consistently.

• Background:
  warm cream (#FCF8F2)

• Primary:
  luxury maroon gradient

• Accent:
  warm gold

• Radius:
  18–20px

• Shadow:
  very soft
  never heavy

• Borders:
  almost invisible

• Plenty of whitespace

• Elegant spacing

• Premium typography

• No oversized fonts

• Keep everything clean.

The UI should feel like:

- Shopify POS
- Square POS
- Apple Store POS
- Toast POS

NOT like a traditional ERP dashboard.
